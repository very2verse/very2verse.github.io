**Review:**<br>
First reaction after reading the book: i miss my boyfriend. Even the long distance 😭😭 hits home<br>
also, as someone who also "lost her virginity" (loosing your virginity is a social construct, reject the patriarchy) later in life and didn't have her first boyfriend until 26, damn it hits close home<br>
i don't think this book's for evryone, probably came at a good time for me, a couple of months from turning 27 and only recently found my footing in the "relationship" department, but it was really light and quite fun to read. i like the emphasis on the internal struggle of Phoebe and somehow i didn't see the "plot twist" coming (although if i had used a bit of critial thiking i could've expected it). sweet and fun to read, nice!
