- Books!!!
    my tech rotation: 
    - *Kindle Oasis, aka Ophelia*: got it as a Día de Reyes present from my dad when i was still in uni. i had an old one that i got when i was 13yo? i think (we all got one in the family and i used it a bunch), but the plastic was sticky and it was literally so slow it was unusable. i wanted to get back into reading and this helped me a lot hehe got it linked to my email so i didn't even have to connect it to my computer to send books. now it's kinda too precious to use on a daily basis? and also the tolino has customisable dictionaries and i read in at least 3 different languages, so Ophelia is safely tucked away with my other tech
    - *Boox Palma*: this was extremely expensive and it was a huge mistake. i thought i could listen to audiobooks or chill music on the background while reading books on different apps, depending on where i have the book, including my library books. turns out the battery drains super fast, it glitches when you try to use more than one app at once, and the ghosting is no joke. idk if i got a faulty one but dammn, i'm hoping to resell it if i get the chance, cause i don't even want it in my home anymore
    - **Tolino Shine Color**: color!! i love seeing the covers in color, makes me wanna read more. i've been using it at home with no case, super light and easy to use. only bad thing, sometimes it does crash out and it takes a bit to reboot
    - **Xteink 4**: new addition, this is everything i wanted the Boox Palma to be. yeah, it has no Android and no backlight, honestly doesn't matter for the price. 40€ for an ereader that i can just toss inside of my bag and it literally weighs nothing. it's so comfortable, so tiny, and with the Crossreader/Crossink software that adds bookmarks, screenshot, reading stats and customisable buttons, it's just sooo goood!! amaze amaze amaze
    - **Innioasis Y1**: got this bad boy while it wasn't as popular and risked the fishy website. took a looong time to arrive but its so nice! I got the custom software (Rockbox) with a [Minecraft theme](https://themes.innioasis.app/theme.html?theme=Minecraft) and put some stickers on it. It looks so pretty!! I don't use it for music as much rn, mostly because organizing an evergrowing music library is a pain in the ass and there's some file names and stuff i haven't figured out how to fix, but i listen to my Audiobooks on there and it's really light to carry around.
